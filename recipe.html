Easy Ice Cream

it's the best dessert made by Gods

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of sour cream
  • 1 gelatin of any flavor

Method of preparation

  1. make the gelatin and don't take the fridge
  2. still hot put in a blender together with condensed milk and sour cream🥵
  3. beat well🥊
  4. freeze❄️
  5. after frozen beat in an electric mixer until creamy😵
  6. back to freeze🥶

    7. it's soft and melt in the mouth and the taste is just the best you can prove
